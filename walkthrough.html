What to say first

One-minute opener

Consentext AI Gateway lets a person choose how a sensitive request is handled before any AI work runs. The choice is not just model quality. It sets the boundary, whether anything may leave, what gets minimized, whether approval is required, and how the result comes back with audit and provenance.

Best live path

Recommended demo sequence

1

Start on Private anchor

Open the live prototype and frame Private as natural-language help without sending sensitive record work outside.

2

Show the four lanes

Explain Deterministic, Private, Private+, and Max Intelligence as different trust and routing choices, not cosmetic presets.

3

Open boundary review

Use the review step to show what stays inside, what may leave, and whether approval is required.

4

Run the result

Show how the answer, provenance, and audit log change with the lane rather than only changing the wording.

5

Switch to Private+

Use the reduced payload preview and approval gating to explain the bridge-lane story clearly.

6

End on Deterministic

Reinforce that the fact-first lane is a real product choice and the trust anchor of the system.
